clicktoeditwrapper.js
A wrapper around a goog.editor.Field that listens to mouse events on the specified un-editable field, and makes the field editable if the user clicks on it. Clients are still responsible for determining when to make the field un-editable again. Clients can still determine when the field has loaded by listening to field's load event.
